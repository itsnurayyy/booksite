About Us

The first Martin's Bookshop was founded in 2003 by two brothers on Republic Avenue. In 2019, when the bookstore was on the verge of disappearing, two friends Nuray and Mustafa bought a bankrupt store with their last money.

After updates, repairs and creating an online website in the conditions of modern technology, in April 2020 they made an official opening on the Kazakh market. Such famous Russian writers, professors as Galymzhan Kurmangaliyev, Ilmaz Nurgaliyev, Meruert Alonso, Akbar Nurlan honored us by visiting our opening, thereby making this day even more significant in the history of our bookstore.

We’re proudly ‘independent’, which, at its simplest, means not being subject to a central buying policy, so we can select the books that we know our customers (who we value beyond measure) will really appreciate.

Since 2014 the bookshop has become a huge success. We have almost 20,000 titles on the shelves and over 200,000 available to order. We are also proud of our art supplies department, and our very popular art workshop one-day courses.We have wonderfully loyal customers who brighten our days, and are so pleased to be in business in this lovely town in the middle of beautiful Wiltshire.



Our Story & Vision

Martin's Book - largest chain of bookstores in the Akmola and Almaty
regions of Kazakhstan. The chain stores are part of one of the largest
bookselling associations in our country – the retail chain "Martin".
We offer a large selection of books, maintaining long-term and friendly
partnerships with most publishers operating in the Kazakhstan book market.
We love books and appreciate the love of our customers for books, so we try
to do everything possible so that each book finds its reader.
